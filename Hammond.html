Richard Hammond

About

Richard Hammond is a well-known English television presenter and former racing driver.

He's best known for his work on the popular motoring shows Top Gear and The Grand Tour.

Hammond is known for his enthusiastic and often humorous personality,

and his love of fast cars and thrilling adventures.

He has been involved in several high-profile car accidents,

but has always recovered and continued his career.

Jeremy Clarkson

About

Jeremy Clarkson is an English television presenter, journalist, and farmer.

He is best known for his work on the motoring shows Top Gear and The Grand Tour.

Clarkson is known for his outspoken and often controversial opinions,

as well as his love of fast cars and luxury brands.

He is a skilled driver and has a reputation for being a demanding and challenging host.

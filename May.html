James May

About

James May is an English television presenter, journalist, and author.

He is best known for his work on the motoring shows Top Gear and The Grand Tour.

May is known for his calm, methodical, and often sarcastic demeanor.

He is a skilled mechanic and has a deep knowledge of cars and automotive engineering.
